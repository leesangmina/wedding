01   About

Architect of
quiet intervals.

김제천 (Kim Je Cheon) is an architect exploring the space between people, program, and place — designing buffers where informal knowledge, coexistence, and everyday encounters can take form.

경계의 틈에서 공존의 가능성을 탐구합니다. 오피스, 호스피스, 공동주거, 학교, 전시공간에 이르기까지, 사용자의 개성과 도시의 결을 함께 담아내는 건축을 지향합니다.

Currently at GANSAM Architects & Associates, Seoul. Previously with Haenglim and Kidan Architecture. 11× student competition awards (2023–2024), and a national STEM excellence scholar.

02   Selected Work

Six projects,
one thread.

From a digital-nomad office in Daegu to a concrete-mesh apartment in Seoul and a fashion office in Italy — each project asks how buffer space can invite coexistence.

/ 01

Nomads Valley Digital nomad office

온-오프를 넘나드는 생활의 현대인들을 위한 유희하는 오피스 리모델링

Three building masses reorganized around a central outdoor garden. The project introduces Agile Workplace — an office choreographed for accidental encounter, where 45° buffer spaces fold informal knowledge-sharing into the workflow of a Multi-Persona generation.

Daegu2023Renovation
/ 02

Urban Sponge Village hospice

환자의 개성을 담은 호스피스 주거가 모여 형성하는 마을

A hospice reimagined as a village. Corridors become gol-mok-gil — alleyways between small homes — and each patient unit gains its own buffer to the shared madang. A quiet architecture for dignity, for solitary elders and children alike.

Coastal Site2023Healthcare
/ 03

Concrete Veins House for all

모두의 집 : 내일의 지구를 위한 오늘의 건축

Renovation of an aged concrete apartment into a cross-species habitat. Bacterial concrete self-heals its cracks; a steel mesh carries niches for owls, sparrows, reptiles, butterflies. The city's veins of concrete become living capillaries of green — KAFF 2024 exhibited.

Seoul · Namsan2024Team · Leader
/ 04

Flexible Balcony Social housing

산업단지 노동자들의 생애주기에 따라 확장되는 집합주거

Six unit types that expand and recombine with a household's lifecycle. Extended balconies carry the residents' colors out to the street façade — indirect communication between migrant workers, students, and the city passing by.

Daegu · Dalgubeol-daero2024Housing
/ 05

Rhizome School Campus elementary school

점심시간과 쉬는시간에 우연히 마주치며 지식을 공유하는 초등학교

Six grade-based learning hubs scattered like a village along the urban and nature axes. A compact-city campus that lets lunch breaks and recess do what curriculum cannot — grow a student's horizontal network of knowledge.

Daechang2024Education
/ 06

One Carpet Fashion office renovation

이탈리아의 패션코드 '아쥬르 에 마로네'를 담은 건축

A single azure fabric drapes through the old brown-brick mill — Azzurro e marrone, the colors of sky and earth — unifying exhibition, workshop and meeting into one continuous catwalk. One gesture, six programs.

Biella, Italy2024Team · Leader
